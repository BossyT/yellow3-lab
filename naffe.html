A yellow3 lab platform

The first practical implementation of Outcome Architecture.

Most software still assumes that humans operate systems.

People click buttons.

Navigate interfaces.

Move information between applications.

Manage workflows.

Coordinate execution.

naffe begins with a different assumption.

What if software could execute, and humans could focus on direction?

That question sits at the centre of yellow3 lab's research into Outcome Architecture - the design of systems where humans define objectives, constraints, and judgment while increasingly autonomous systems handle execution.

naffe is the first large-scale implementation of those ideas.

From software operator to system director

Traditional software requires continuous human operation. Even modern AI tools often reduce to a better interface for the same work. The user still operates the system.

naffe is designed around a different relationship.

Instead of learning software, users describe outcomes.

Instead of configuring workflows, they define objectives.

Instead of managing tools, they direct systems.

The platform translates that direction into software, workflows, agents, and operational capability.

The goal is not to make software easier. The goal is to make software less necessary.

Built for non-technical founders

Throughout the history of software, the people with the problems and the people capable of building the solutions have rarely been the same people. That gap has prevented countless ideas from becoming reality.

naffe was created to close that gap. Founders, operators, and business leaders should be able to describe what they need in plain language and receive working systems in return.

Not mockups.

Not specifications.

Not requirements documents.

Working outcomes.

Human direction remains central

Autonomy does not remove humans from the process. It changes their role.

The most important decisions remain human:

Purpose

Priorities

Constraints

Judgment

Accountability

The system executes. The human directs.

This principle sits at the heart of both naffe and the broader research agenda of yellow3 lab.

Why we built it

Research is only useful if it survives contact with reality.

naffe exists as a living laboratory for the ideas explored at yellow3 lab. Every build, every interaction, every successful outcome, and every failure contributes to a deeper understanding of how humans and increasingly autonomous systems can work together.

Research informs practice.

Practice informs research.

naffe is where that cycle becomes visible.
