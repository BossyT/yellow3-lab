About yellow3 lab

yellow3 lab is an AI research studio based in Copenhagen, Denmark. Founded by Thomas Christian Melskens, yellow3 lab designs and develops AI-powered platforms that eliminate complexity in software creation and digital product development.

Mission

yellow3 lab exists to delete the barriers between ideas and reality. For decades, building digital products required developers, agencies, and months of work. yellow3 believes AI changes that equation fundamentally — not by making tools easier to use, but by removing the need for tools entirely. The best technology is the technology you forget is there.

Research Focus

yellow3 lab conducts applied research in three core areas: AI Agent Architecture — developing multi-agent systems that collaborate autonomously to interpret intent, generate output, and ensure quality. Design Intelligence — teaching AI to produce professional-grade design, moving beyond templates to genuine design thinking. Prompt-to-Product Systems — bridging the gap between natural language descriptions and working digital products.

Platforms

yellow3 lab develops AI platforms for industries where complexity blocks progress. The lab's first platform enables users to describe software in plain language and receive production-ready applications — built, deployed, and working — with no code, no designers, and no agencies required.

Partnerships

yellow3 lab partners with forward-thinking companies exploring what AI makes possible for their industry, customers, or operations. The lab works with organizations that want to explore AI's potential, not just execute a specification.

Location

yellow3 lab is headquartered in Copenhagen, Denmark, and operates globally.
