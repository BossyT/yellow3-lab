About yellow3 lab

yellow3 lab is an independent research and venture lab based in Copenhagen. We research how humans work with increasingly autonomous systems, and how that relationship is reshaping software, organisations, markets, and society.

Alongside our research, we actively build AI-native products and companies. Research informs practice. Practice informs research.

This lets us discuss AI not as observers, but as practitioners - operating at the intersection of business leadership and real-world implementation.
