# Provider logo sources

Provider marks are the genuine brand logos in their official colours, stored
locally and unmodified, shown only to identify each provider next to its models
(nominative use). Source: Lobehub Icons — https://github.com/lobehub/lobe-icons
(MIT). OpenAI and xAI are shown in their official monochrome form. Verified
2026-07-13. Providers without an available official mark (e.g. Xiaomi, Poolside)
use a neutral initials tile. Logos are trademarks of their respective owners.
