Executive summary

Five conclusions, three minutes. What changed across model routing, economics and the European picture in July 2026.

  1. Asia holds the lead at 63.9% of routed tokens (+9.4 pp over four weeks) - the concentration of demand by region of origin is not easing.
  2. Anthropic Claude Sonnet 5 was the month's biggest climber, up 32 places to #12.
  3. 3 tracked model(s) cut price this month, extending the cost gap between low-cost challengers and premium frontier models.
  4. 6 new model(s) entered the ranking, led by Tencent Hy3 (free).
  5. Europe remains marginal at 0.45% of routed traffic; the regulatory picture is tracked in Europe Watch below.
